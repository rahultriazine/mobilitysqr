Terms of Your Assignment

This Agreement confirms the terms and conditions of the Assignment. Key Assignment details, including project name, project location, and beginning and end dates (the “Assignment Start Date” and “Assignment End Date”) are detailed in the Assignment Summary above. Please note that both the Assignment Start Date and Assignment End Date may be subject to change should MobilitySqr India in its absolute discretion consider it necessary and any reference to the Assignment Start Date or Assignment End Date in this Agreement will be a reference to the revised date in the event that either is changed.

Beginning on the Assignment Start Date you will move off the payroll of MobilitySqr Consulting Private Limited (“MobilitySqr India”), and for the duration of the Assignment (the “Assignment Period”) you will be on the payroll of MobilitySqr Limited (“MobilitySqr UK”). At the end of the Assignment you will return to India and will be reinstated on the MobilitySqr India payroll.

 

 

 

Base Compensation

As of the Assignment Start Date, and for the duration of the Assignment Period, you will be paid the annualized initial base compensation (“Initial Base Compensation”) specified in the Assignment Summary.  This Initial Base Compensation is simply your current MobilitySqr India salary converted into UK Pounds Sterling.  In addition to the Initial Base Compensation, if necessary for compliance with UK wage laws you may also receive an increase in your base salary; if so, this amount will be specified in the Assignment Summary (the “Additional Base Compensation”).  If no UK Pounds Sterling amount is filled in for Additional Base Compensation in the Assignment Summary above, you will not be receiving any Additional Base Compensation.   Your total annualized base compensation (“Total Base Compensation”) is specified in the Assignment Summary, and is simply your Initial Base Compensation plus your Additional Base Compensation (if any).  Your Total Base Compensation will be paid to you through the MobilitySqr UK payroll on a monthly basis.  Information concerning the exchange rate used to calculate your Total Base Compensation is available on the GPM site on the MobilitySqr People Portal.





Allowance

In addition to your Total Base Compensation you will be paid the tax-free allowance (the “Tax Free (Host Country) Travel Allowance”) specified in the Assignment Summary.  As your Assignment is intended to last between 61 and 180 days (inclusive), this Tax Free (Host Country) Travel Allowance is intended to contribute to your routine daily living expenses incurred in carrying out the Assignment except for housing.  Additional information on expense policies, including those expenses for which you may file an additional request for expense reimbursement, is provided on the GPM site on the MobilitySqr People Portal.  The Tax Free (Host Country) Travel Allowance will be paid to you through the MobilitySqr UK payroll on a monthly basis.  

At the end of the Assignment, you will return to the MobilitySqr India payroll, and the Additional Base Salary and any Tax Free (Host Country) or Taxable (Host Country) Travel Allowance will end.





Travel and Accommodation

Your travel arrangements to and from the UK will be made through the GPM Team, at MobilitySqr’s cost.  Travel arrangements will be made pursuant to MobilitySqr India’s standard travel guidelines.  Generally you will be entitled to one “flyback” to India (at MobilitySqr’s cost) for every three months you are in the United Kingdom, in accordance with MobilitySqr’s policies as established from time to time.  If you wish, you may use this “flyback” to have a family member travel from India to visit you in the UK. You can find MobilitySqr's policies on this topic, and additional information concerning flybacks and related travel issues, on the GPM People Portal site.  All such travel arrangements for your Assignment must be made though the GPM Team.

As your Assignment is intended to last between 61-180 days (inclusive), MobilitySqr UK will provide you with housing in the form of a hotel, corporate apartment or similar accommodation.  Your Project Contact specified in the Assignment Summary will provide additional information concerning your housing.  Please note that you will be bound by MobilitySqr UK's Accommodation Policy.





Benefits

During the Assignment health insurance for you while on Assignment (including your family if they travel with you) will be covered under the International SOS Travel insurance plan.  If you or your family, currently have health insurance through MobilitySqr India, such coverage will continue in full force and effect, subject to the terms of that coverage.

If you currently participate in the Provident Fund, you will not be eligible to participate in it during your Assignment; however, we believe the combination of Total Base Salary and Tax Free (Host Country) or Taxable (Host Country) Travel Allowance paid to you will compensate for this temporary pause.  Please note that you will not be eligible to participate in the MobilitySqr UK pension plan while on your Assignment in the UK.

Finally, if you are participating in a MobilitySqr India bonus plan as of your Assignment Start Date, your participation in such plan will continue during the Assignment, subject to the specific terms of that plan.  Unless otherwise specified in writing, you will not be eligible for participation in any other MobilitySqr UK benefits or bonus plans.

If you are currently participating in MobilitySqr’s {{AssigneeName}} Stock Purchase Plan (“ESPP”), during the Assignment Period you will be eligible to continue your contributions through the MobilitySqr UK payroll (if you are not currently participating in the ESPP, you will have an opportunity to do so at the beginning of the next offering).  At this time, the ESPP permits a qualified participant to purchase MobilitySqr Consulting common stock at a 15% discount through payroll deductions of up to 10% of a participant’s cash compensation.  If you have any questions about MobilitySqr India benefits you should contact your GPM Team contact or the MobilitySqr India PSO.  Questions about MobilitySqr UK
benefits and policies should be directed to your UK People Strategist.

Unless specifically set forth in this Agreement, your MobilitySqr India benefits as of the Assignment Start Date will continue in full force and effect.  Please note that all references in this Agreement to MobilitySqr India’s or MobilitySqr UK’s option plans, vacation policy, other policies and procedures, medical, dental, disability and life insurance plans, bonus or compensation plans, ESPP, and other benefit programs are to those plans, policies, procedures and programs as they are currently in effect and/or as any of them may be modified at any time in the respective company’s sole discretion.   In the event of any conflict between information contained in this letter and in any of the plan, policy or program documents, the plan, policy or program documents will control.
